## Plot Overview:

**Pottel** is a story set in a small village in Telangana during the 1980s. The village is ruled by **Patel (Ajay)**, who doesn't want anyone to get an education. On the other hand, **Ganga (Yuva Chandra)** is a shepherd who cares deeply for his goat, Balamma Pottel. Ganga's goal is to educate his daughter and bring justice by exposing Patel's wrongdoings. However, one fine day, Balamma Pottel is taken for a sacred sacrifice in the village itself where the Hero was really shocked to hear it.
